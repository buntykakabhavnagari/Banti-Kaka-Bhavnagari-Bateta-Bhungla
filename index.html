The Legacy

A Story of Passion

After spending 16 years working in a pharmaceutical company, Bunty Kaka decided to leave his high-paying job and follow his passion for food. In 2024, he started serving Bhavnagar’s famous Bhungla Bateta, bringing the authentic street taste to people. His goal was simple — serve delicious food and make every customer happy.

Since the beginning, he has personally taken feedback from over 1,000+ customers to continuously improve the taste and experience. Today, Bunty Kaka’s Bhungla has become a favorite spot for people who love the true flavors of Bhavnagar & Treat all them as a family
